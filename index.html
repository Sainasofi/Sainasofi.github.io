Saina Sofi

Saina moves through the ordinary world with ease, but beneath that calm surface lies a mind drawn to the shadows. Her poetry collections, Hushed Aches and From the Attic, unravel the quiet complexities of human emotion, while their fiction ventures deeper into the unknown. With A Tapestry of Time and The Cursed Bloodline, Saina explores the darker threads of existence—where the supernatural seeps into reality, and every secret has a pulse of its own. Her fascination with the eerie took root in childhood, shaped by creaking floorboards, half-glimpsed figures, and the unsettling feeling that something was always watching. Through stories woven with both fragility and fear, Saina invites readers to step beyond the veil and linger in the spaces where reality grows thin.
